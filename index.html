Will switching suppliers disrupt my business operations? +
No — not at all. The physical supply of electricity doesn't change. The same wires, same grid, same reliability. What changes is the company billing you. The switch happens entirely in the background and is complete within 48 hours of signing. Your operations continue without any interruption whatsoever.
Do I need to provide my energy bill upfront? +
No bill needed for your initial review. We can estimate your saving based on your sector, state and rough monthly spend. If you'd like a more precise analysis, sharing your bill later helps us identify specific savings like demand charge reductions — but it's never required upfront.
How much does your service cost? +
Nothing. Our service is 100% free to you. We're paid a small commission by the energy supplier when you switch — this doesn't affect your rate or add any cost to your bill. Our interests are aligned with yours: the more you save, the better our relationship. There are no hidden fees, ever.
What's a demand charge and could I be paying one unnecessarily? +
Demand charges are fees based on your single highest power draw in a billing period — not total usage. Many businesses on the wrong tariff pay these unnecessarily. It's one of the biggest savings opportunities we find, especially for manufacturers, auto shops and warehouses. Our audit specifically checks for this.
What if my current contract hasn't expired yet? +
We review your contract as part of the free audit. In many cases, we can lock in a better rate to begin when your current contract ends — at no cost. In cases where the savings are large enough, early exit fees may still be worth paying. We calculate this for you clearly, with no pressure to proceed.
Which states do you operate in? +
We currently serve businesses in Pennsylvania, Texas, Illinois, Ohio and New Jersey — all fully deregulated energy markets where businesses can choose their supplier. We know each state's utility landscape, rate structures and regulatory requirements inside out.
How long does the full switch take? +
Once you agree to proceed, the switch is complete within 48 hours for electricity and 5–7 business days for gas. You don't need to do anything — we manage the entire process with your new supplier and notify you when it's live and your new rate is active.
