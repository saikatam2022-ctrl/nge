Our Story
Built by people who know where businesses get burned

We started from a simple observation: most commercial businesses have no idea what they're actually paying for energy — or what they could be paying instead.

Our founders spent years working in commercial energy procurement and watched the same pattern repeat itself. A business owner, too busy running operations to scrutinise their energy contract, rolls over onto a variable rate at the end of their fixed term — and quietly overpays for years. The supplier banks the margin. The business never knows.

We built Next Gen Energy to fix that. As an independent broker with no ties to any single supplier, we compare rates across more than 20 electricity providers in every deregulated state we serve. Our audit process is forensic — we look for demand charges, wrong tariff classifications, and renewal traps that businesses routinely miss entirely.

The result: average savings of 18% on annual energy costs, delivered in a 10-minute review, with the full switch completed in 48 hours. Free — because we are paid by the supplier, not by you.

"Our interests are perfectly aligned with yours. The more you save, the better we look — and the longer you stay a client. There is no scenario where padding your bill benefits us."
